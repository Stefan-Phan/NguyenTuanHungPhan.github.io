FIRST YEAR COURSES
INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Information Technology, involving computer systems and networks, drives innovation and efficiency across various industries.

Information Technology Fundamentals

This course provides a comprehensive overview of the fundamentals of Information Technology. Topics covered include computer systems, data management, software development, and the impact of IT on society.

UO Design Thinking and Digital Innovation

This course focuses on the principles of design thinking and how they apply to digital innovation. Students will learn problem-solving techniques, user-centered design, and how to create innovative solutions using digital technologies.

Network Fundamentals

This course covers the basics of computer networking, including network architecture, protocols, and network security. Students will gain hands-on experience in setting up and configuring computer networks.

Problem Solving and Programming

This course introduces students to the fundamentals of programming and problem-solving techniques. Topics include algorithm design, programming languages, and software development methodologies.
